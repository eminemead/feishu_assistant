## Key Concepts

- **P&L (Profit & Loss)**: Financial statement showing revenues, costs, and expenses
- **Revenue**: Total income from business operations
- **Profit**: Revenue minus costs
- **Loss**: When costs exceed revenue
- **EBIT**: Earnings Before Interest and Taxes
- **Margin**: Profit as a percentage of revenue
